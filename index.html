ABOUT?
The website is all about Call of Duty Mobile (CODM), one of the most popular mobile shooting games in the world. It serves as an information hub for CODM players, where they can find guides, the latest new tips and tricks, weapon builds, and updates about tournaments and events. The goal of the website is to create a community space for CODM gamers to learn, connect, and improve their gameplay.
